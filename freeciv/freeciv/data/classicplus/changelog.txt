Brief Explanations of goals and challenges.

Make a Classic+ ruleset for people who want a faithful recreation of the Classic game, but without
the flukes and imbalances that haven't aged so well. 2x movement to give oxygen to game pace and
provide starting experience for modern multiplayer games. A few more units, wonders, or buildings
to spice things up, but no changing the meat of the game.
